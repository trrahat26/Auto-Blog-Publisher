# Free Monetization Setup Checklist

1. Publish these pages as Blogger Pages:
   - About
   - Contact
   - Privacy Policy
   - Disclaimer

2. Keep the page URLs exactly:
   - /p/about.html
   - /p/contact.html
   - /p/privacy-policy.html
   - /p/disclaimer.html

3. Connect Google Search Console and submit sitemap:
   - https://YOURBLOG.blogspot.com/sitemap.xml

4. Keep posting consistently for 30 days.

5. Apply for AdSense from Blogger Earnings tab.

6. After approval, replace `ad-slot` placeholder blocks in theme with real AdSense units.
